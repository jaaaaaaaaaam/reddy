About reddy.js

reddy makes each section of your site at least the height of the window.

With reddy you can section off parts of your website. It is used to make 100% height sections of your site.

If the section's first child is longer than the height of the browser, the script does nothing. However, if it is smaller, it extends the height of the section to make it as tall as the browser window.

This is useful for directing people to a certain section of your site.

Using reddy.js

So, how do i turn this thing on?

Using reddy is as simple as including a .js after you have defined your sections.

Setting up sections

<section>
    <div class="container">
        Your content goes here.
    </div>
</section>

<section>
    <div class="container">
        This is the second "page"
    </div>
</section>

Include the script at the bottom of your page.

<script src="js/reddy.js" type="text/javascript"></script>

Finally, initialise the script!

<script type="text/javascript">
    $(function()
    {
        $('body').reddy();
    });
</script>
